Dream Places Bucket List

Japan

I’ve always wanted to visit Japan because of its perfect mix of tradition and modern culture. Akihabara especially stands out to me because I love anime, games, and tech, it feels like a paradise for those interests.

Beyond that, I want to see sakura trees and the peaceful temples in Kyoto, experience the organized chaos of Tokyo, and try authentic ramen. Japan just feels like a place where my interests are at their peak.
